Bio

ALBUM OF THE YEAR - "NOBODY FITS"!
Thanks to the love and support of our unbeatable friends and fans, we were able to release our 3rd studio effort! Produced by Grammy Award winner, CHAD BROWN. Our efforts were rewarded this past year by winning the Nashville Industry Music Awards - Album of the Year!

Voted Nashville Industry Music Awards 2013 Best Pop Rock Band and Best Live Rock Performers. Also nominated for Artist of the Year and Album of the Year.

Voted Nashville's 2011 “MUSICIANS OF THE YEAR” by RAW: Natural Born Artists, BLACK MARKET RESEARCH is an Indie/Alt band headquartered in Nashville, TN.

Formed in 2009 as the brainchild of Nick Britt, the band gives credit for their popularity to exposure gained while performing with acts such as Three Dog Night, Kelly Clarkson, Stryper, Florida Georgia Line, the Gin Blossoms, Sponge and Tiffany.

In January 2011, BMR released their EP, “Insult to Injury”, which found it's way onto local radio stations and helped create a loyal following in Middle Tennessee. The album caught the attention of Grammy-winning producer Adrian David Payne, who produced the band's first music video.

BMR wasted no time recording their debut album “Shapeshifter's Masquerade”, which was released globally December 2012.

BMR music can be purchased on iTunes, Amazon.com, Rhapsody, Spotify, and Zune.

Join BMR's mailing list at www.blackmarketresearch.net to receive updates about the band and tour dates.

Black Market Research is proud to be endorsed by KAM Instruments, Carvin / Kiesel Guitards, and True Honor clothing.
