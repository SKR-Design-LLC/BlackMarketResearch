April 13th 2016:

“Friday the 13th is generally regarded as a day of misfortune and superstition. But this year it marks the official global digital release of our album, NOBODY FITS. It will be available for download on iTunes and Amazon or you can stream it on Spotify, Google Play, etc...

Our song "PTSD" will be available as a free download in hopes that you share it with those who need to hear it. I don't expect the song the save the world- but the Vet who told me to write it said there are a lot of people who need to know they're not alone.

The rest of the album has plenty of songs about girls, love, career choices, anxiety, divorce, and infatuation. This one took a long time. I hope you find time to give it an ear👂🤘”
- Nick Britt

Available on Spotify or download it from CDBaby, Amazon and iTunes!

November 28th, 2015 “PTSD” selected as this weeks Buzz Cut on 102.9

“I am beyond honored to have our song “PTSD” selected as this weeks Buzz Cut on 102.9.
(Meaning you can hear it everyday this week at 2pm)

It's a song that's very dear and serious to me because it was inspired by the struggles of our soldiers who return from battle, only to go to war with themselves. My brother in law, who was a combat medic in Iraq and Afghanistan, lost one of his best friends when an IED tore through his Humvee, inflicting fatal wounds and creating emotional trauma in the rest of the company that may never subside. And though the war is “over”, there continues to be a record number of suicides by our servicemen that needs to be addressed. They claw through hell, and when they get home we expect them to “dust it off and carry on.”

The song is bigger than I am because it's all I have to offer as a token of appreciation for their service. I'm overjoyed that 102.9 the buzz has gotten behind this tune to push it. If you get a chance, turn it up.

And thank a Vet…”
- Nick Britt

The Update
(6/5/15)

“I got to hear some rough cuts off the forthcoming record, and I wanted to extend my gratitude to the phenomenal recent contributions by: percussionist MATT BURGESS (featured work with Willie Nelson,Lynyrd Skynyrd,Big & Rich,Gretchen Wilson,Thompson Square,Jewel,Velvet Revolver,Cindy Morgan,Bryan Duncan,O’Shea,Ty Herndon,Steel Magnolia,Drake White,Matt Kennon,James Otto,Trent Tomlinson,John Michael Montgomery, Robert Randolph).

The new record features cellist DAVE EGGAR (who has composed and recorded with Phillip Phillips, Tony Bennett,Andrea Bocelli, Frank Ocean, Talib Kweli, Evanescence, Chromeo, RJD2, Fall Out Boy, Patti Smith, Michael Brecker, Chris Potter, Dave Sanborn, John Pattitucci,Mark Jackson, Amy Winehouse, 208 Talks of Angels, Paul Simon, James Taylor,OAR, Robert and Bylle Redford, Beyonce, Taylor Swift, Coldplay, A Great Big World, Breaking Benjamin, Five for Fighting).

And my newest hero, who is equally as humble as he is talented, guitarist DOUG LANCIO, who has played with Patty Griffin, Hootie and the Blowfish, Steve Earl, John Hiatt, Martina McBride, David Allen Coe, Jim Lauderdale, Holly Williams, Jennifer Knapp, and Will Hoge.

It is with awe and sincere appreciation that I say thanks for giving me a chance to record my songs with these huge players who have made me more proud of this record than any piece of music I've been part of before.

And thanks to my friends for funding this project.

The record will be on it's way to LA next week with producer CHAD BROWN to have some keyboards and organs added to it. I cannot wait to show you the work we've put into it.”
